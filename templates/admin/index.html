eKonek

Customer-Worker Services Bridge

This application contains listing of workers ready to accept job requests from customers. Both customers and workers will use a mobile app to request/accept jobs.

The customer request jobs using the Customer App by providing the location and service required. The mobile app will show the list of skilled workers for the selected service. The workers will use the Worker App to accept the job and read the details.
